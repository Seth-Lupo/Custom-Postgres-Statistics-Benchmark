Schema Notes for StackOverflow Database:

Table: posts
- creaiondate (with typo, not creationdate)
- lasactivitydate (not lastaccessdate)
- lasteditdate
- closeddate
- communityowneddate

Table: users  
- creationdate (correct spelling)
- lastaccessdate (not lasactivitydate)

Table: comments
- creationdate (correct spelling)

Table: posthistory
- creationdate (correct spelling)

Table: votes
- creationdate (type: date, not timestamp)

Table: badges
- date (not creationdate)

Table: postlinks
- creationdate (correct spelling)

Table: tags
- No date columns
